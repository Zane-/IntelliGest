IntelliGest Getting started

Adding new gestures

To add other gestures, click the "Add Gesture" dropdown menu, and click the action you would like to add. A new box will appear on the screen representing that gesture.

Beginning training

Once you have added at least one other gesture, you can begin training. Click the "Start training" button to continue.

Adding examples

After the "Start training" button is pressed, a new "Add example" button should appear, allowing you to add examples from your webcam for each gesture. Tap and hold to continuously add examples. Capture different angles and positions to add accuracy. The more examples the better, but more memory is used.

Do nothing gesture

The "Do nothing" gesture is the one required gesture. It will perform no browser action. This gesture should simply be what your webcam view is when you do not intend to execute any gestures.

Training and Testing

Once you are finished adding examples, click "Train model" to train the neural network. Be careful! All your examples will be reset, so you will have to start over if you want to make changes or add another gesture. To test the gestures, click "Test Model".
